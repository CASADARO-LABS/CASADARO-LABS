About Us

Custom SoundProof Labs specializes in creating custom offices and media production spaces with a focus on focus.

We work closely with clients to design and build spaces that meet their specific needs, requirements, and Asthetics.

Focus and Sound Proof Offices

Our focus offices are specially designed workspaces intended to provide a distraction-free and acoustically isolated environment. Key features include:

  • Sound absorbing wall and ceiling panels to minimize echoes and dampen external noise
  • Solid core doors with seals to block sound transmission
  • Sound-rated windows (if any) and extra insulation in walls
  • White noise machines or sound masking systems to cover up remaining low level noises
  • Minimalist decor to reduce visual distractions

Media Production Spaces

Our media production spaces are designed to meet the unique requirements of audio and video production. Key features include:

  • Recording studios with sound isolation booths, diffusers, bass traps and acoustic treatment
  • Mixing/mastering rooms with carefully calibrated acoustics and high-end audio monitoring equipment
  • Video production spaces with green screen cycloramas, lighting grids, sound stages, and control rooms
  • Specialized electrical and HVAC systems to handle the equipment load
